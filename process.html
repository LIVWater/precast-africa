How It Works

From brief
to handover.
One team.

Four stages, six steps, one team accountable for every module. Design, manufacture, transport, install, project-managed end to end.

The majority of construction activity is shifted into a controlled factory environment — reducing site dependency, weather risk, and quality variability.

Four Stages In Detail

Every step. Inspected. Documented.

Every module passes through four distinct delivery stages, each with its own QA, documentation, and developer sign-off.

01

Design & Engineering

4–6 weeks
Lifecycle steps 01Design Brief 02Engineering

Apartment and housing layouts are converted into a modular building system using repeatable module types, structural design, service coordination, and installation planning.

  • Modular layout planning
  • Structural engineering
  • MEP coordination
  • Module connection design
  • Lifting & transport planning
  • Installation sequencing
  • Quality-control planning
  • Project-specific documentation
02

Factory Manufacturing

6–8 weeks
Lifecycle steps 03Production

Modules are cast and fitted in a controlled production environment, improving consistency, reducing waste, and allowing quality checks before delivery to site.

  • Mould preparation
  • Reinforcement placement
  • Concrete casting
  • Controlled curing
  • Dimensional checks
  • Embedded services & fittings
  • Selected finishes
  • Factory quality inspections
03

Transport & Installation

3–4 weeks
Lifecycle steps 04Transport 05Installation

Completed modules are transported to site and installed using planned lifting, positioning, connection, and sealing procedures — coordinated with site programme.

  • Module loading & dispatch
  • Transport logistics
  • Crane & lifting coordination
  • Placement onto foundations
  • Module alignment
  • Structural connection
  • Service connection
  • Joint sealing & QA checks
04

Final Integration & Handover

1 week
Lifecycle steps 06Handover

Modules are connected, services are integrated, joints are sealed, and final works are completed to deliver a finished residential building ready for occupation.

  • Final service commissioning
  • Waterproofing inspections
  • Finishing works
  • Compliance checks
  • Quality assurance sign-off
  • Defect inspection
  • Handover documentation
  • Warranty registration
