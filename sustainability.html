Green & Sustainable Concrete

Lower carbon. Same strength.

Concrete is the primary material in the PPVC system, so improving its environmental performance is central to our sustainability strategy. We prioritise the development of lower-carbon mixes that maintain the strength, durability, fire resistance, and structural performance required for volumetric construction.

  • Lower-carbon concrete mix design
  • Cement reduction & engineered optimisation
  • Supplementary cementitious materials (fly ash, slag, calcined clay)
  • Recycled aggregates in non-critical applications
  • Admixture optimisation for strength and durability
  • Factory batching control & waste recovery
  • Per-module carbon tracking

Business Sustainability

People. Skills. Supply chains.

Sustainability is a commercial discipline as much as an environmental one. We build resilience into the company — through plant utilisation, supplier diversification, skills development, and governance reporting from day one.

  • Financial resilience scaled to secured offtake

  • Standardised modules driving plant utilisation

Sustainability KPIs

We measure what we ship.

From the first project, performance is tracked across material efficiency, energy, water, waste, quality, safety, and community impact.

Carbon

Embodied carbon per module

Tracks the carbon impact of each module produced over time.

Cement

Cement content & replacement %

Monitors embodied-carbon intensity and lower-carbon cementitious uptake.

Waste

Waste generated per module

Measures manufacturing efficiency and waste reduction performance.

Strength

Concrete strength compliance

Ensures sustainable mixes still meet all structural performance requirements.

Water

Water used per module

Tracks production water efficiency across batching and curing cycles.

Energy

Energy used per module

Plant energy efficiency tracked per module and per cycle.

Local

Local supplier percentage

Measures local economic participation across the supply chain.

Quality

Defect rate at handover

Measures product quality and durability outcomes at the unit level.

Safety

Safety incidents

Tracks worker safety and operational responsibility on factory and site.
