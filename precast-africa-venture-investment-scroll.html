The Demand

2.4 million homes short. 80,000 units missed every year. The gap that defines the opportunity.

An irreducible signal
Market Demand

A backlog
that compounds

South Africa's housing deficit has grown every year for a decade. Conventional construction is structurally incapable of closing it — there are not enough bricklayers, not enough months in a year, not enough sites to deploy them on.

PPVC is the only proven technology that changes the production equation. It moves the build from the field to the factory, and from manual to industrial scale.

Chapter 02

The Technology

Factory-built. Crane-installed. Field-proven. Spanish-engineered moulds, South African delivery.

Inside the factory
PPVC — defined

Three-dimensional
concrete modules

Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction produces complete 3D concrete modules — walls, floors, ceilings, finishes — in a controlled factory environment.

Modules are transported to site and stacked by crane. Build time on site: days, not months. Quality is repeatable because it is industrial, not artisanal.

Moldtech (Spain)

Spanish precision,
built for Africa

Moldtech is a Spain-based specialist designing steel moulds for industrial concrete module production. Their moulds are deployed globally — and engineered for our Phase 1 configuration.

Each mould produces one cast per day across 255.5 productive days per year. Fixed Type A moulds for standard modules. Modular Type B & C for variable configurations.

Mould preparation

Steel mould cleaned, release agent applied, reinforcement steel cage placed. Cycle time: ~2 hours per cast.

2

Concrete pour

High-strength concrete batched on site, poured into mould. Embedded inserts for services, doors, windows.

3

Cure & demould

24-hour controlled cure cycle. Mould opened, module lifted out by gantry, moved to finishing bay.

4

Finish & QA

Plumbing, electrical, plaster, fittings installed factory-side. NHBRC quality inspection.

5

Transport

Abnormal-load truck moves module within 30km. Crane offloads at site.

6

Stack & seal

Modules stacked, joints sealed, services connected. Three-storey building erected in a week.

PPVC moves the build from the field to the factory. Once you do that, every variable that breaks construction projects — weather, labour, theft, sequencing — collapses into one process you can actually control.

The Offtake

Demand is signed, not projected. The Mixilon agreement converts speculation into contracted revenue.

The bankable foundation
Mixilon (Pty) Ltd

A named buyer.
A captive pipeline.

Mixilon is a residential developer with an active pipeline at Droegjevel Estate, Randfontein — within Greater Johannesburg. They have committed a R1 million commercial setup payment and a 5–10 year exclusive offtake.

This is what bankability looks like. Lenders don't fund factories on hope. They fund factories whose output is already contracted.

SPV Structure

Clean,
protected,
bankable.

Investment is made into a dedicated Special Purpose Vehicle that owns the factory assets, mould equipment, and offtake contract rights.

The waterfall returns capital first, then preferred return, before profit share. Operating partners only participate after investors are fully repaid.

Profit distribution

The waterfall

  • 1
    Operating costs & debt service

    All factory OPEX, Moldtech maintenance, transport, admin, and bank debt interest/principal paid first. DSCR minimum 1.4× maintained.

  • 2
    Investor capital repayment

    100% of R48.3M investor equity returned from free cash flow before any profit share. Target repayment: 2028.

  • 3
    Preferred return — 12% p.a.

    Investors receive 12% preferred return on outstanding capital balance from day 1, accrued and paid alongside capital repayment.

  • 4
    Profit share — post return

    After full capital + preferred return: remaining profits split 50/50 between Investor Pool and Operating Partners.

  • 5
    PA / Mixilon split — scaled

    Within the operating share: initial 50/50 converts to 70/30 in favour of Precast Africa once 500 unit/year run-rate is achieved.
