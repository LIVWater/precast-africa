Commercial Principles

Built on clear terms.

Every Precast Africa engagement runs on a predictable commercial structure — transparent module specifications, fixed design-freeze dates, milestone-based payments, and clear allocation of responsibilities.

  • Clear module specificationsStandardised module dimensions, finishes, and tolerances locked in writing before manufacture — no ambiguity at delivery.
  • Fixed design-freeze datesDrawings sealed by a contracted date so factory slots, materials, and cost are protected against late-stage changes.
  • Milestone-based paymentsCash flow tied to verifiable production and delivery milestones — not generic monthly drawdowns.
  • Agreed production schedulesFactory slots reserved against your programme, with shared visibility into casting and curing cycles.
  • Site-readiness obligationsFoundations, access, services, and crane standing space defined upfront so installation days run on time.
  • Escalation clauses on inputsTransparent indexing of cement, steel, and labour movements — with caps and triggers agreed in advance.
  • Cranage & logistics responsibilitiesWho provides crane, rigging, transport, and access — written into the contract, never assumed on site.
  • Quality & acceptance criteriaDocumented checks at factory dispatch and site receipt, with photo evidence and joint sign-off.
  • Retention limitsCapped retention amounts and clear release triggers — no open-ended holdbacks distorting cash flow.
  • Warranty terms documentedDefined warranty period, scope, and remedy process — covering both manufactured modules and installation.
