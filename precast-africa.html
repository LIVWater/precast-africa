Company Overview

Transforming construction across Africa

Precast Africa is a sustainable precast construction-system company built around one mandate: help developers deliver more homes, faster, with greater predictability and less waste. We design, manufacture, transport, and install — with full accountability from brief to handover.

Through our PPVC system, we shift the majority of construction activity from site to factory. Precision-engineered reinforced concrete modules arrive on site ready for crane installation — shorter programmes, fewer defects, less waste, and a building system that scales.

The PPVC System

Repeatable. Configurable. Precision-built.

The Precast Africa PPVC System uses factory-produced, prefinished volumetric reinforced concrete modules — each transported to site and installed by crane onto prepared foundations. Every module is engineered for structural safety, fire resistance, acoustic separation, and long service life.

Three primary module families combine to create any residential configuration. Standardisation drives production efficiency; controlled customisation serves developer and market requirements.

Each module: ~6m × 2.4m × 3m. Factory-prefinished with MEP, windows, doors, and selected finishes. Installed with a 30-ton mobile crane. Structurally connected and service-commissioned on site.

Module Type A

Main Module

Primary habitable space. Configurable for studio, 1-bed, and 2-bed layouts. Flexible window arrangements, door positions, and internal partition options within structural limits.

Module Type B

Wet-Core Module

Centralises bathrooms, kitchens, plumbing, drainage, and electrical services. Factory-tested before dispatch. Eliminates the most common defect source in conventional residential builds.

Module Type C

Stair / Core Module

Vertical circulation, stair access, structural core, and service shafts for multi-storey buildings. Provides lateral stability across the full stacked structure. Includes single-flight and return-stair configurations for low- and mid-rise blocks.

How It Works

From factory to finished building

The Precast Africa system shifts the majority of construction activity off-site — then delivers precision-built, finished modules directly to your development, ready for crane installation.

01

Design & Engineering

Stage 01
Design & Engineering

Module configuration, structural engineering, MEP co-ordination, and programme planning are completed off-site before production begins. Design is anchored to your project brief.

02

Certification & Approval

Stage 02
Certification & Approval

Structural certification, fire compliance, acoustic performance, and building authority approvals are progressed in parallel with production. No waiting for site.

03

Factory Production

Stage 03
Factory Production

Modules are cast in 3D volumetric steel moulds in a controlled production facility. Reinforcement, embedded services, fittings, windows, and selected finishes are installed before the module leaves the factory.

04

Transport & Logistics

Stage 04
Transport & Logistics

Completed modules are loaded onto engineered transport frames and delivered to site in installation sequence. Delivery is coordinated with crane access, the site programme, and foundation readiness.

05

Crane Installation

Stage 05
Crane Installation

Modules are lifted by mobile or tower crane, positioned onto prepared foundations, aligned, structurally connected, and service-connected using engineered installation procedures and trained installation teams.

06

Commissioning & Handover

Stage 06
Commissioning & Handover

Services are tested and commissioned, statutory inspections are completed, and the building is handed over with full QA documentation, warranties, and as-built records.
